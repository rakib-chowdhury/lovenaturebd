Port-Blair-Havelock-kolkata (Andaman islands)

7 Days & 6 Nights

Tour Itinerary:


Day 01 : Dhaka-Kolkata Start journey from Dhaka kollanpur bus stand  10:00 pm. By AC Bus. Overnight Bus.

Day 02: Kolkata-Receive from Kolkata Bus stand ( 2 pm) in the noon. Check in a Hotel. Room for Bath & relax. After Lunch free movement and shopping  by guest cost :  Overnight Kolkata.

Day 3: Arrival at Port Blair After Breakfast drop to kolkta airport , & Airport at Arrival at Port Blair in the morning by flight & transfer to your hotel. After lunch, visit the Corbyn's Cove Beach - 9 kms from Port Blair - a coconut palm fringed beach, ideal for swimming, sun-basking & bathing. Then proceed to visit the famous Cellular Jail followed by the Light & Sound show in which the saga of freedom struggle, brought alive in the show. Drop back at the Hotel. Overnight Port Blair.

Day 4:  Port Blair- Havelock Port Blair ( Day Excursion)
Early morning departure ferry from Port Blair Havelock. The ferry takes 2.5 hrs to reach Havelock. Half Day Sight Seeing to Beach No 7* (Radhanagar Beach) rated as the Best Beach in Asia by Time Magazine (N/Ac Car will be provided for pickup and drop. Same day return and overnight at Port Blair. Overnight Port Blair.
 
Day 5: North Bay and Ross Island Excursion.
Morning breakfast, tour to full- day excursion to North Bay and Ross Island. Experience the enthralling under water marine life, coral reef, beautiful ornamental fish etc, amidst a vast stretch of tropical forests, silver sand and blue waters...live the breathtaking bygone ear British Empire at the former administrative island. Return back from the Island... Recollect and relax. Overnight Port Blair.

Day 6: Departure drop at Airport,After Breakfast Leave with lovely memories to cherish. Airport drop Port Blair. Receive Kolkata Airport & Transfer to  Hotel. Evening free movement and shopping by guest cost . Overnight Kolkata.

Day 7: Kolkata to Dhaka,After Breakfast drop to Bus Stand
