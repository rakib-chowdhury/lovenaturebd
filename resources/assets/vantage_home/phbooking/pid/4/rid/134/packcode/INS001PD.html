Shillong-Cheerapunjee
6 Days 5 Nights


Itinerary:

Day 01: Starts drive for Shillong at 12pm from Dhaka. Overnight Bus journey.


Day 02: After having breakfast complete immigration formalities in Dawki Border (Tour start) & 3 hours scenic drive to Shillong. In the evening free time for Shopping, Dinner & night halt at Shillong, India. Overnight Shilong.

Day 03: Daylong sightseeing at Shillong & visit 8 points (Shillong Pick, Elephant Falls, Barapani Lake, Lady Hydari Park, Wards Lake, Bishop Beadon Falls, Wards Lake, Golf Ground), In the evening free time for Shopping. Dinner & night halt at Shillong, India. Overnight Shilong. 

Day 04: After early breakfast go for whole day Cheerapunjee sightseeing. Visit 6 points (Visit Nohkailkai Falls, kohramong view point, Mousmai Cave, Mawkdok Valley, Ramakrishna Mission, Eco Park, Nohsngithiang/ Seven sister Falls, Thangkharang Park, Motrop. Come back to Shillong in the evening. O/N Shillong. Overnight Shilong.

Day 05: Start drive for Bangladesh at 8 am, on the way will visit Living Root Bridge, Cleanest Village Lunch will be served on the way . Complete immigration formalities & in the evening Sylhet. Dinner & starts drive for Dhaka at 12am. Overnight Bus journey.

Day 06: Early Morning Reach Dhaka.
